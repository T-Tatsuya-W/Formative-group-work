Using Simulations at work

Nowadays working environments become more and more welcoming to new professionals and people who want to raise their skills. Big part of this is thanks to rapidly developing technological area with its realistic (and not just that) simulations.

Image by Sanket Prabhu; accessed from: ARreverie

AR and VR

When talking about simulations, we don't just mean technologies like virtual and augmented realities (VR and AR). Simulations that run without additional hardware (like portable devices that support AR and helmets) are used even more widely. This is caused by them being more affordable and accessible. The VR and AR simulations provide an even more engaging environment for the user.
